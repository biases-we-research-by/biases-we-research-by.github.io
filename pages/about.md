<h1 style="margin-bottom: 20px; color: #1a3e5c;">Biases We Research By</h1>
    
 <p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 20px;">
      Biases We Research By is a tutorial on detecting and mitigating understudied forms of bias in NLP research. 
      It combines social science insights, practical methods, and perspectives from data labelers to provide a clear understanding of bias in NLP.
    </p>
    
<p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 20px;">
      The tutorial is co-located with <strong>LREC 2026</strong> (11–16 May) in Palma de Mallorca, Spain.
</p>
    
<p style="font-size: 1.1rem; margin-bottom: 20px;">
      It will be held in the <bold>afternoon session</bold> (14:00–18:00) on 11th May in Room 4.
</p>
    
<p style="font-size: 1.1rem;">
      No specific requisites are required. It is an interdisciplinary workshop spanning social sciences to NLP.
</p>
